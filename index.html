The Story

The TV is on.
Nobody is watching.

Authentic Studios is not a fashion brand. It is a question. The retro TV on this tee is not decoration — it is a mirror. Aimed at everyone who consumes without thinking. Who follows without choosing. Who wears what they are told.

We took heavyweight cotton, spliced it with camo, destroyed it by hand, and printed a collage that asks the question most people are too comfortable to ask: Where is my mind?

This is the first piece. Print on demand — made when you order, not before. No excess. No warehouses. No shortcuts. Just the work.
